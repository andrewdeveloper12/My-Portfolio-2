About me

Hello, my name is Andrew Osama, a dedicated web designer specializing in front-end development with a strong foundation in computer science. I have a passion for creating visually appealing. My skill set combines creativity with technical expertise, allowing me to build experiences that are both engaging and efficient.

Currently, I’m focused on enhancing my skills in Angular and React, which has enabled me to deliver faster, more interactive web experiences. I pride myself on my attention to detail and my ability to transform complex concepts into simple, impactful designs. A project I’m particularly proud of is an interactive food-related website, where I dynamically displayed images based on user interactions. This challenging project allowed me to push the boundaries of my creativity, leaving a lasting impression on the client.

Growing up, I was always curious about how things worked—whether it meant pulling apart gadgets or figuring out how websites are constructed. That curiosity evolved into a passion for web development, where I continue to learn and innovate with each project. With every website I create, I aim to bring ideas to life in a way that resonates with users, combining aesthetics with functionality for memorable digital experiences.
